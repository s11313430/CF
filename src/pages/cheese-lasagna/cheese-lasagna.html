Cheese Lasagna

INGREDIENTS

15 oz ricotta 4 c mozzarella, shredded 1/2 c parmesan, grated 2 eggs, slightly beaten 25 oz marinara sauce 1 pkg lasagna noodles prepared following package directions Add All Ingredients DIRECTIONS Mix all of the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese with the eggs in a large mixing bowl.

Spray 13x9 baking dish with cooking spray.

Place 3 to 4 lasagna noodles over bottom of pan, slightly overlapping.

Spread 1/3 of the cheese and egg mixture over the noodles and then cover with 1/3 of the marinara sauce.

Place 3 to 4 lasagna noodles and remaining marinara sauce. Cover with remaining 1/3 of cheese and egg mixture.

Cover pan with foil and bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.

Remove the foil from the pan and bake for another 10 minutes.

Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
